Hurricane Preparedness

What to Do During a Hurricane

  • Stay indoors and away from windows.
  • Seek shelter in a windowless interior room.
  • Have emergency supplies and a charged phone ready.
  • Evacuate if instructed by authorities.

Emergency Kit Essentials

  • Flashlights, batteries, and extra clothing.
  • Food, water, and personal hygiene supplies.
  • Radio and phone charger.
