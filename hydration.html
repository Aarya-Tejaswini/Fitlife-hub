Tips for Nutrition and Hydration

  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Focus on nutrient-rich foods, including whole grains, lean protein, fruits, and vegetables.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to prevent dehydration, which is common in older adults.
  • Smaller, More Frequent Meals: Eating smaller meals more frequently can help with digestion and maintain energy levels.
  • Monitor Salt Intake: Too much salt can lead to high blood pressure. Opt for low-sodium options where possible.
