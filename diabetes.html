Healthcare Tips for Diabetes

  • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly: Keep track of your glucose levels to prevent spikes and drops.
  • Eat a balanced, low-sugar diet: Focus on whole foods, avoiding sugary snacks and processed foods.
  • Exercise regularly: Aim for 30 minutes of moderate exercise each day.
  • Consult with your doctor regularly: Stay on top of your medication and treatment plan with regular check-ups.
