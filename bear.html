Self-Defense Against Bears

Learn how to protect yourself in the rare event of a bear encounter.

How to Defend Yourself Against Bears

If you encounter a bear, these steps may help you stay safe:

  • Stay calm and try to make yourself appear larger by raising your arms or opening your jacket.
  • Speak in a calm but assertive voice to let the bear know you're human.
  • Back away slowly, but do not run. Running may trigger a chase response from the bear.
  • If the bear charges, use bear spray (if available) to deter the bear from attacking you.
  • If no bear spray is available, try to climb a tree or get behind a large object to put distance between you and the bear.
