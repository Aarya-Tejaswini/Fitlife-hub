Mental Health Tips for Children

  • Encourage Open Communication: Foster an environment where children feel comfortable expressing their thoughts and feelings.
  • Promote Positive Self-Esteem: Encourage your child to try new things and praise their efforts to build confidence.
  • Physical Activity: Regular exercise can reduce anxiety and depression, so encourage your child to play and stay active.
  • Limit Screen Time: Reduce excessive screen time to promote face-to-face interactions and better sleep quality.
  • Monitor Behavior Changes: Pay attention to signs of anxiety, sadness, or withdrawal, and seek professional help if necessary.
