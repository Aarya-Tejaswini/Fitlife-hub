Breast Cancer Prevention and Early Detection Tips

    Self-Examination: Regularly perform breast self-exams to detect changes in the breast tissue. Learn the proper techniques for performing a self-exam.

    Mammograms: Schedule routine mammograms as recommended by your doctor, especially if you're over 40 or have a family history of breast cancer.

    Healthy Lifestyle: Maintain a healthy diet and engage in regular physical activity. Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they are risk factors.

    Know Your Family History: If you have a family history of breast cancer, discuss your risk with your healthcare provider and consider genetic counseling.

    Stay Informed: Stay updated on the latest research about breast cancer prevention, early detection, and new treatments.
