Types of Workouts for Women

1. Cardio Workouts

Description: High-energy cardio exercises such as jump rope, running, and cycling.

Benefits: Improves heart health, burns fat, and increases stamina.

2. Strength Training

Description: Resistance exercises focusing on building muscle mass with weights and machines.

Benefits: Builds lean muscle, boosts metabolism, and enhances overall strength.

3. Yoga Workouts

Description: Relaxing yoga routines designed to increase flexibility and mindfulness.

Benefits: Improves flexibility, reduces stress, and promotes mental clarity.

4. HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Description: Short, intense bursts of exercise followed by brief periods of rest.

Benefits: Burns fat, increases metabolic rate, and improves cardiovascular fitness.

5. Pilates Workouts

Description: Core strengthening exercises focusing on posture, alignment, and flexibility.

Benefits: Tones muscles, improves posture, and reduces back pain.
