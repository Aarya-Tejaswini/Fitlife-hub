Tips for Preventing Falls

  • Exercise Regularly: Strengthening and balance exercises can improve stability and prevent falls.
  • Home Modifications: Remove tripping hazards, install grab bars, and ensure adequate lighting.
  • Wear Proper Footwear: Choose shoes that provide good support and traction to prevent slipping.
  • Regular Vision Checkups: Vision problems can lead to falls, so have regular eye exams.
