Self-Defense Against Animals

Against Dogs

Stay calm, avoid eye contact, and back away slowly. If attacked, protect your face, chest, and throat.

Against Bears

Stand your ground and avoid running. Use bear spray or make yourself appear larger if necessary.

Preparedness for Natural Disasters

During Earthquakes

Drop, cover, and hold on under sturdy furniture. Stay indoors until the shaking stops and avoid windows.

During Floods

Move to higher ground immediately. Avoid walking or driving through floodwaters. Keep emergency supplies handy.

During Hurricanes

Evacuate if instructed. Stay in a windowless interior room on the lowest level. Keep emergency kits ready.

During Wildfires

Evacuate immediately if advised. Wear protective clothing and a mask to avoid inhaling smoke.
