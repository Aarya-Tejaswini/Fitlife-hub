Recommended Vaccination Schedule for Children

    Birth to 6 Months: Hepatitis B, DTP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, and Pertussis), Hib (Haemophilus influenzae type b), IPV (Polio), PCV (Pneumococcal), RV (Rotavirus).

  • 6-18 Months: DTP, Hib, IPV, MMR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella), Varicella (Chickenpox), Hepatitis A.
  • 4-6 Years: DTP, IPV, MMR, Varicella.
  • Adolescents (11-12 Years): Tdap (Tetanus, Diphtheria, Pertussis), HPV (Human Papillomavirus), Meningococcal vaccine.
  • Flu Vaccination: Annual flu vaccination for children over 6 months old.
