Self-Defense Against Dogs

Learn how to protect yourself if you encounter an aggressive dog.

How to Defend Yourself Against an Aggressive Dog

If you are ever confronted by an aggressive dog, here are some practical steps to stay safe:

  • Stay calm and avoid making sudden movements.
  • Do not make eye contact with the dog, as it may interpret that as a threat.
  • Try to back away slowly and steadily without turning your back on the dog.
  • If the dog attacks, protect your face, chest, and throat, and try to use an object to shield yourself, like a backpack or jacket.
  • If available, use pepper spray or any other deterrents to stop the dog.

What to Do if Bitten

  • Stay calm and try to get away from the dog as quickly as possible.
  • Seek medical attention immediately to prevent infection and ensure proper care for the bite.
