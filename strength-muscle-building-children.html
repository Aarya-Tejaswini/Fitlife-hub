Strength & Muscle Building

1. Bodyweight Exercises

Description: Push-ups, squats, and lunges tailored for kids.

Benefits: Builds core strength and develops muscle coordination.

2. Resistance Bands

Description: Safe resistance training with lightweight bands.

Benefits: Increases flexibility and muscle endurance.

3. Animal-Based Movements

Description: Fun exercises like bear crawls and crab walks.

Benefits: Improves overall strength and enhances balance.

4. Climbing Activities

Description: Safe climbing exercises at home or parks.

Benefits: Strengthens arm and leg muscles while improving focus.

5. Medicine Ball Exercises

Description: Lightweight medicine ball workouts for kids.

Benefits: Develops coordination and enhances core stability.
