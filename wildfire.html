Wildfire Preparedness

What to Do During a Wildfire

  • Evacuate immediately if instructed by authorities.
  • Wear protective clothing and a mask to avoid inhaling smoke.
  • Keep windows and doors closed to prevent smoke entry.
  • Stay tuned to emergency alerts and updates.

Emergency Kit Essentials

  • Protective clothing, goggles, and masks.
  • First aid kit and necessary medications.
  • Flashlights, water, and food supplies.
