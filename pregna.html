Pregnancy Care Tips

    Regular Prenatal Checkups: Schedule regular doctor visits to monitor the health of both mother and baby.

    Healthy Nutrition: Maintain a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, including folic acid and iron.

    Exercise: Stay active with light exercises such as walking or swimming to improve circulation and reduce stress.

    Avoid Harmful Substances: Avoid smoking, alcohol, and excessive caffeine to ensure a healthy pregnancy.

    Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to keep your body hydrated and support your baby's growth.
