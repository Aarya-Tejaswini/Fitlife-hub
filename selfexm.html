How to Perform a Breast Self-Exam

  • Stand in front of a mirror and look for any changes in the size, shape, or color of your breasts.
  • Feel your breasts while lying down and standing up, using the pads of your fingers to cover the whole breast.
  • If you notice any unusual lumps or changes, contact your doctor immediately.
