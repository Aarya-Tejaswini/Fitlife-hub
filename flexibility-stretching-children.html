Flexibility & Stretching for Children

1. Basic Stretching Exercises

Description: Simple stretches to increase flexibility and range of motion.

Benefits: Improves posture, reduces muscle stiffness, and enhances overall flexibility.

2. Yoga for Kids

Description: A kid-friendly yoga session to stretch and improve flexibility.

Benefits: Increases balance, reduces stress, and enhances flexibility.

3. Dynamic Stretching

Description: A series of movements to increase flexibility and prepare the body for exercise.

Benefits: Increases range of motion, prevents injuries, and prepares muscles for activity.
