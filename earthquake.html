Earthquake Preparedness

What to Do During an Earthquake

  • Drop, cover, and hold on under sturdy furniture.
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects.
  • If outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings and trees.
  • After shaking stops, check for injuries and follow evacuation protocols.

Emergency Kit Essentials

  • First aid kit, flashlight, batteries, and water.
  • Non-perishable food and important documents.
  • Portable charger and radio for communication.
