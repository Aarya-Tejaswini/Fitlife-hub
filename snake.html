Learn how to protect yourself when encountering a snake.

How to Defend Yourself Against Snakes

In case you encounter a snake, here are some steps to follow:

  • Stay calm and keep still to avoid provoking the snake.
  • If you spot the snake, do not approach it, and move away slowly.
  • Do not try to handle or kill the snake; instead, give it space to slither away.
  • If youâ€™re hiking, wear thick boots and pants to reduce the chances of a bite.

What to Do if Bitten

  • Call for medical help immediately. Time is critical in snake bite situations.
  • Try to remain as still as possible to slow the spread of venom through your body.
  • Do not attempt to suck out the venom or apply a tourniquet, as these are ineffective and dangerous.
