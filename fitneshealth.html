Top Fitness Tips

  • Incorporate at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily.
  • Find a workout routine that you enjoy and can stick to.
  • Strength train at least twice a week to maintain muscle health.
  • Stay consistent with your exercise schedule for long-term results.
  • Warm up before and cool down after workouts to prevent injuries.
  • Listen to your body and take rest days when needed.
